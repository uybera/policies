# Deleting your My Stream account

## Procedure for requesting account deletion

Users of the **My Stream** app can request the deletion of their account by sending an email to the following address:

**buyar@belfininternational.be**

In this email, please specify the email address associated with the **My Stream** account so that the request can be processed.

## Data Deleted

When the account is deleted, the personal data associated with the user account is deleted in accordance with the user’s request.

## Data Retained

Certain data may be retained to ensure the service continues to function or to enable the restoration of user preferences:

- The user account’s email address
- Articles saved by the user
- Notification rules configured by the user

## Additional retention period

Retained data remains stored only for as long as is necessary for the purposes mentioned above. Any permanent deletion of retained data will be carried out in accordance with applicable legal obligations and **My Stream**’s data retention policies.
